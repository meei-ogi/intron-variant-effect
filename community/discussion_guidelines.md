## Purpose
This repository discussion space is intended for scientific exchange, feedback, questions, and interpretation of results related to the project.

## Appropriate topics
- Questions about methods and analysis
- Interpretation of results
- Suggestions for improving analysis pipelines
- Discussion of related literature
- Reporting potential issues or inconsistencies

## Not appropriate
- Off-topic discussions unrelated to the dataset or analysis
- Unsupported claims without data or references
- Personal opinions unrelated to scientific content
- Medical advice or clinical decision-making

## Evidence and references
When making claims or interpretations, please include supporting references (e.g., PubMed, DOI, or dataset IDs). Speculation should be clearly marked as such.

## Discussion tone
- Be respectful and constructive
- Focus on data-driven arguments
- Clearly distinguish hypothesis from conclusion
- Avoid overstating findings beyond what the data supports

## Reporting issues
If you identify potential errors in analysis or data interpretation, please open an issue and provide:
- File or figure reference
- Description of the concern
- Suggested correction (if available)


**This repository is for research purposes only and does not provide medical advice or clinical recommendations.**
